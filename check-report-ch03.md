## Adversarial Pass

**Biggest overstatement in chapter:** The sentence in the claim-03-1 section — *"如果你等到传统意义上'接收到饱的信号'才停下来，基本可以认定你已经吃多了"* — is stated as a near-certainty but rests solely on the author's own framing. The 15–20 min satiety-delay figure cited in claim-03-1's card comes from elsewhere (not this source directly), and the "基本可以认定" phrasing may overreach for a general population claim. The chapter itself notes this is "书中作者的直接表述（综合）", which is the correct mitigation, but the bold formatting in the prose gives it unearned rhetorical force.

**Strongest skeptic counterargument:** For claim-03-5, a skeptic could note that the two cited null-result RCTs both used brief interventions on healthy-weight or lab-constrained populations, which may underpower real-world mindful-eating programs. Meta-analyses of longer mindful-eating interventions (e.g., Carrière et al., 2018, Obes Rev) do find reductions in binge/emotional eating. The chapter's conclusion ("可能有助于") is appropriate and defensible, but the framing slightly undersells the emotional-eating sub-finding (some support does exist) relative to how hard it dismisses the weight-loss claim.
